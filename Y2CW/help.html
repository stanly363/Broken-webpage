Help & Documentation

How to Browse Movies

Use the genres listed on the left to filter movies by genre. Click on "View Movie" to see more details.

Adding Movies to Basket

Select the number of days you wish to rent the movie and click "Add to Basket".

Viewing and Managing Your Basket

Click on "View Basket" to see items in your basket. You can remove items or proceed to checkout.

Proceeding to Checkout

From your basket, click "Proceed to Checkout" and fill in your payment details to complete your order.

Contact Us

If you need further assistance, please contact us at support@movierentals.com.
